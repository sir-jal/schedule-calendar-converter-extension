Course Categorization
The extension will categorize courses based on the checkboxes below. A course with multiple categories will be placed in the one with the highest priority.

Places courses with no meeting time in their own category. Min: 1, Priority: 1
Places waitlisted courses in their own category. Min: 1, Priority: 2
Places courses with the same course code in their own category. Min: 2, Priority: 3
