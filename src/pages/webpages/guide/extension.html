Chrome Extension Guide

Intro

This page serves as a guide for using the Chrome Extension.

Guide

Using the extension is quite simple! Click on a dropdown menu below to learn more.

Ellucian Banner

This extension only works with Ellucian Banner.

To check if your school uses Banner, go to your school's registration page. If the link ends with: /StudentRegistrationSsb/ssb/registration, then your school uses Banner. You may proceed to the rest of this guide!

How to Use

To use the extension, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your school's registration page.
  2. Click on "View Registration information". This is the page that contains your current and past schedules.
  3. Ensure you're on the term you want to export.
  4. Click on "Schedule Details".
  5. Open the extension and click "Load Schedule".
  6. Customize your file. Use this guide to help you.
  7. Export your file.
  8. Done!
Your progress will be saved

If you accidently close the extension while still customizing your .ics file, the extension will catch it. Upon reloading it, the extension will give you an option to continue where you left off.
