File Customization Guide

Intro

This page serves as a guide for customizing your .ics file once you import your classes. This applies to both the extension popup and the import page.

Guide

There are many ways to customize your .ics file. Click on a dropdown menu below to learn more.

Per course settings

Each course has its own settings. These settings are:

  • Include course code
  • Include professor names
  • Only include primary professor
  • Include section
  • Include class location

Some of these settings may be disabled due to the lack of their corresponding information.

To toggle a setting, simply click on its checkbox.

Schedule Settings

The schedule itself also has settings. These settings are located at the bottom of the course list. Below are the schedule settings:

  • Include waitlisted courses

To toggle a schedule setting, simply click on its checkbox just like you would a course setting.

Settings that affect other settings

Below are the settings that affect other settings:

Include waitlisted courses (schedule setting)

  • If disabled, all waitlisted courses will be excluded.

Include professor names (class setting)

  • This setting will affect all professor-related settings.

Toggling a parent setting will affect its child settings. You will see this in action in the UI.

Renaming a class

You may rename a class. This is especially helpful if you have multiple classes with the same name. For example, at Georgia Tech, a math course typically has a lecture, studio, and exam block. Each block can have the same name. Renaming these courses can help you differentiate them.

How to Rename

A course can only be renamed if it's included. To rename a course, you may either:

  • Double click the course, or
  • While focused on the course, click on one of the following keys on your keyboard:
    • F2
    • E

A textbox will appear. Use that to rename the course. Once you rename it, either click enter or click out of the textbox to save the name.

If you leave the textbox blank, or if the name is blank after omitting forbidden characters (see below), the name will return to the orignial course name.

Forbidden Characters

The following characters are not allowed in course names and will be omitted if they are detected:

  • \ (backslash)
  • , (comma)
  • ; (semicolon)
  • Extra spaces, including those that are
    • at the beginning of the name
    • at the end of the name
    • between words
  • New line characters

These characters could cause issues when importing your file(s) into calendars such as Google Calendar and Outlook.

Bulk Settings

You may bulk change settings by using the buttons and dropdown menu located under the schedule settings. Courses with no meeting information (indicated by a red tint) and settings that are disabled will not be affected by bulk changes.

Overlapping settings still apply.
