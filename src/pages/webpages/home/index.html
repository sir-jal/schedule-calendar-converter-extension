Welcome to SCC

Schedule to Calendar Converter is an extension created by Sir Jal, a student, to help other students transfer their classes to their digtal calendar(s) of choice. With a click of a few buttons, you can export your Banner schedule as an .ICS file and quickly add your courses to your calendar.
This extension only works on Ellucian Banner!

What's new in 2.0.2

  • Extension settings have been added
    • You may change the behavior of the extension.
    • Each save has a 15-second cooldown.
    • Ensure the date format is the same one your school uses. Otherwise, you will have trouble when exporting your .ics file.
  • Course categories (if activated) now allow you to bulk include courses
  • You may now export your schedule as a .JSON file
    • The import page also accepts .JSON.
    • These .JSON files could be useful for debugging errors.
    • You CANNOT import these files into a calendar.
