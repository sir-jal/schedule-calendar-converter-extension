Welcome to SCC

Schedule to Calendar Converter is an extension created by Sir Jal, a student, to help other students transfer their classes to their digtal calendar(s) of choice. With a click of a few buttons, you can export your Banner schedule as an .ICS file and quickly add your courses to your calendar.
This extension only works on Ellucian Banner!
