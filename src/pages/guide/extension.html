Chrome Extension Guide

Intro

This page serves as a guide to using the Chrome Extension. Click here to view the guide home page.

Guide

Using the chrome extension is quite simple! To get started, ensure that your school utilizes Ellucian Banner as its registration system. If this is not the case, unfortunately this extension will likely not work for you. To check whether or not your school is compatible with the extension, check to see if your registration page's link ends with:
/StudentRegistrationSsb/ssb/registration


After verifying that your school is compatible, follow these steps:

  1. Go to your school's "view registration information" page. This is the page that shows your current and past courses.
  2. Once on the page, choose the term you want the extension to export. In most cases, you will already be on this term.
  3. Load the extension.
  4. Click on "load schedule" to import your schedule into the extension.
  5. Customize your .ics file. Visit the guide for file customization to learn more.
  6. Click on the export .ics file button. This will cause your system to prompt you to name your file and select its location.
  7. Voila! You may now use the file to add your classes to your calendar of choice.
